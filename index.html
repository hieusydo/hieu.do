ABOUT

Hey there, thanks for checking out my unborn baby. As you can see, I am still a web-design noob. If you have any advice or know any resources, let me know! My email is in the Contact section. You can also spam me with a tirade of how awful this site is lmaoo

Anyway, my name is Hieu. (I know, who even has four vowels in his name?) It's pronounced like Hugh. Not hard right?

I am a freshman at NYU, majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. I like both software and hardware. I want to contribute to the creation of some AI form like Jarvis. I also want to make a durable multi-functional intelligent suite. Ok, yes I want to become Iron Man. What's wrong with that?

I usually let myself sink into deep thoughts (it's more like random philosophical bs), and I feel like I have realized many things about life and universal humans' conditions, but most of the time I don't do well explaining myself to others (do you even know what I have been saying?), so I'll let my favorite quotes represent different pieces of my mind. Hopefully you can connect them and have a general idea of who I am.
